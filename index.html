Real examples

Templates that get replies.

Not generic. Not GPT-generated. Written from real conversations that turned into real deals.

Connection Request
Hi [First Name],

Saw your post about [specific topic] — good take on [one point].

I work with [industry type] across [region]. Thought it worth connecting.

— [Your name]
First Message After Connect
Hi [First Name], thanks for connecting.

Quick question — are you currently looking at ways to [achieve X outcome] for [their business type]?

I've been working with [similar company type] on this and have a few ideas that might be relevant.

Worth a 10-min chat this week?
Follow-Up #2 — Value Add
Hey [First Name],

Came across this [article/stat/insight] about [their industry] today — thought you'd find it useful.

[One sentence summary of why it's relevant to them]

Still happy to share what we've been doing if useful.
The Honest Close (Final Touch)
Hi [First Name],

I know you're busy. Happy to close the loop here — is this simply not a priority right now, or is the timing off?

Either way, no problem at all. Just want to make sure I'm not missing something on my end.
